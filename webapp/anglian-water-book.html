Anglian Water

In October 2018, the UK SAP CX team requested support to to deliver a presentation for an RFP Demo Day at Anglian Water HQ. Whilst the intial brief was simple, we added in a new dimension to the story being told. We created the character of John Drinkwater - a drop of water, and used this character to tell the demo story. We created a custom storytbook that included this characters hydrological journey through the utility company.
