Merlin Entertainments

In March 2019, the UK SAP CX team requested support on a RFP response for Merlin Entertainment in the UK. Having previously worked with the team on other Merlin opportunities we were able to utilise our experience and complete the journey we had taken them on already. We decided to customize our RFP response into a Superhero book. Each one personalised to the recipient with a Lego character.
